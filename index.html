      How this works (high level)

      1. All processing is done in your browser. OCR uses Tesseract.js; portrait matching uses a simple image fingerprint comparison against PNGs you host in this repo under /portraits.
      2. After you click “Send Row to Google Sheet”, the site calls your Google Apps Script Web App, which saves the screenshot to Drive and appends a row to your sheet (columns A–T).
      3. You can always correct any parsed field before sending, so bad OCR never ruins the sheet.
